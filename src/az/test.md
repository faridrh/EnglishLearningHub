### Qısa Təkrar Sualları

### Articles (Artikllər)
**Düzgün artikli seçin:**
1. I saw ___ elephant at the zoo.
   - a) a
   - b) an
   - c) the
   
2. ___ sun is very bright today.
   - a) A
   - b) An
   - c) The

**Cavablar:** 1-b, 2-c

---

### Plural Nouns (Cəm Adlar)
**Cəm formasını yazın:**
1. book → ______
2. child → ______
3. woman → ______

**Cavablar:** 1-books, 2-children, 3-women

---

### Present Simple (İndiki Sadə Zaman)
**Düzgün forma ilə tamamlayın:**
1. She _____ (work) in a hospital.
2. They _____ (not like) coffee.
3. _____ you speak English?

**Cavablar:** 1-works, 2-don't like, 3-Do

---

### Present Continuous (İndiki Davam Zamanı)
**Düzgün formanı seçin:**
1. Right now, I _____ my homework.
   - a) do
   - b) am doing
   - c) does

2. The children _____ in the garden.
   - a) are playing
   - b) is playing
   - c) play

**Cavablar:** 1-b, 2-a

---

### Subject Pronouns (Mübtəda Əvəzlikləri)
**Düzgün əvəzliklə əvəz edin:**
1. John is tall. _____ is my friend.
2. Mary and I are students. _____ study English.
3. The book is interesting. _____ is on the table.

**Cavablar:** 1-He, 2-We, 3-It

---

### Possessive Adjectives (Mənsubiyyət Sifətləri)
**my, your, his, her, its, our, their ilə tamamlayın:**
1. This is _____ car. (mənə məxsus)
2. Where is _____ bag? (sənə məxsus)
3. The cat is cleaning _____ fur.

**Cavablar:** 1-my, 2-your, 3-its

### Qısa Təkrar Sualları

### Past Perfect (Keçmiş Mükəmməl Zaman)
**Past Perfect ilə tamamlayın:**
1. When I arrived, she _____ already _____ (leave).
2. They _____ never _____ (see) such a beautiful sunset.

**Cavablar:** 1-had left, 2-had seen

---

### Modal Verbs (Modal Feilər)
**Düzgün modal seçin:**
1. You _____ wear a helmet when riding a bike.
   - a) can
   - b) must
   - c) might

2. It _____ rain tomorrow.
   - a) should
   - b) must
   - c) might

**Cavablar:** 1-b, 2-c

---

### Passive Voice (Məchul Növ)
**Məchul növə çevirin:**
1. The teacher explains the lesson.
   → The lesson _____ by the teacher.
2. Someone stole my bicycle.
   → My bicycle _____.

**Cavablar:** 1-is explained, 2-was stolen

---

### Conditionals 1 & 2 (Şərti Cümlələr 1 və 2)
**Şərti cümləni tamamlayın:**
1. If it _____ (rain), I _____ (stay) home. (Tip 1)
2. If I _____ (be) rich, I _____ (travel) the world. (Tip 2)

**Cavablar:** 1-rains, will stay  2-were, would travel

---

### Relative Clauses (Nisbi Cümlələr)
**who, which, that istifadə edərək birləşdirin:**
1. The man lives next door. He is a doctor.
   → The man _____ lives next door is a doctor.
2. I bought a book. It was very expensive.
   → The book _____ I bought was very expensive.

**Cavablar:** 1-who, 2-that/which

---

### Reported Speech (Dolayı Nitq)
**Dolayı nitqə çevirin:**
1. "I am tired," she said.
   → She said _____.
2. "Where do you live?" he asked.
   → He asked _____.

**Cavablar:** 1-that she was tired, 2-where I lived

### Qısa Təkrar Sualları

### Subjunctive Mood (Şərti Meyl)
**Şərti meyl ilə tamamlayın:**
1. I suggest that he _____ (go) to the doctor.
2. It's important that she _____ (be) on time.

**Cavablar:** 1-go, 2-be

---

### Inversion (İnversiya)
**İnversiya istifadə edərək yenidən yazın:**
1. I have never seen such a beautiful sunset.
   → Never _____ such a beautiful sunset.
2. She not only sings but also dances.
   → Not only _____ but she also dances.

**Cavablar:** 1-have I seen, 2-does she sing

---

### Cleft Sentences (Bölünən Cümlələr)
**Bölünən cümləyə çevirin:**
1. John bought the car yesterday.
   → It was _____ who bought the car yesterday.
2. I need your help.
   → What _____ is your help.

**Cavablar:** 1-John, 2-I need

---

### Conditionals 3 & Mixed (Şərti Cümlələr 3 və Qarışıq)
**Şərti cümləni tamamlayın:**
1. If she _____ (study) harder, she _____ (pass) the exam. (Tip 3)
2. If I were you, I _____ (accept) that job offer yesterday. (Qarışıq)

**Cavablar:** 1-had studied, would have passed  2-would have accepted

---

### Ellipsis & Substitution (Ellipsis və Əvəzetmə)
**Ellipsis və ya əvəzetmə ilə tamamlayın:**
1. "Are you coming to the party?" "I hope _____."
2. My car is faster than _____ (your car).

**Cavablar:** 1-so, 2-yours

---

### Participle Clauses (Participle Cümlələri)
**Participle cümləsi istifadə edərək yenidən yazın:**
1. Because she was tired, she went to bed early.
   → _____ tired, she went to bed early.
2. The man who is standing there is my teacher.
   → The man _____ there is my teacher.

**Cavablar:** 1-Being, 2-standing
